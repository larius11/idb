Arson

Definition

Arson is a crime of intentionally, deliberately and maliciously setting fire to buildings, wildland areas, abandoned homes, vehicles or other property with the intent to cause damage.

Statistics

There were 156,514 individual arson incidents reported to the FBI in United States between 2006 and 2016 by 7420 law enforcement agencies reporting data.

In 2016, there were 13.3 incidents of arson per 100,000 people
