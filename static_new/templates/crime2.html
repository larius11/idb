Kidnapping

Definition

In criminal law, kidnapping is the unlawful carrying away (asportation) and confinement of a person against his or her will.

Statistics

As of December 31, 2016, NCIC contained 88,040 active missing person records. Juveniles under the age of 18 account for 33,706 (38.3%) of the records and 42,807 (48.6%) records when juvelines are defined as under 21 years of age.

During 2016, 647,435 missing person records were entered into NCIC, an increase of 1.9% from 634,908 records entered in 2015.
